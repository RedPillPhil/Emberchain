# Game Concept: EmberQuest (Working Title)

I'd like to build a 2D side-scrolling platformer inspired by classic Mario games, but designed from the ground up around blockchain ownership and progression rather than simply adding NFTs as cosmetics.

The goal is for the game to be genuinely fun first, with blockchain enhancing the experience instead of feeling forced.

## Core Gameplay

Players run through handcrafted levels while:

* Jumping across platforms
* Defeating enemies
* Discovering hidden areas
* Collecting coins
* Finding secrets
* Completing worlds
* Unlocking achievements

Gameplay should feel smooth and responsive like classic platformers.

## Blockchain Integration

To play, each player owns a **Character NFT**.

This NFT is more than a skin—it represents the player's playable character.

Different character NFTs have different strengths and weaknesses.

Examples:

* Ninja Ember

  * +15% movement speed
  * Slightly lower health

* Tank Ember

  * Starts with 2 HP
  * Slightly slower movement

* Sky Ember

  * Higher jump
  * Lower defense

* Treasure Hunter

  * Increased coin collection radius
  * Slightly reduced jump height

The goal is balance—not pay-to-win. Every character should have advantages and tradeoffs.

## Equipment NFTs

Players can equip NFT gadgets that modify gameplay.

Examples:

* Coin Doubler

  * Doubles all coins collected in a level

* Coin Magnet

  * Pulls nearby coins toward the player

* Shield Generator

  * Absorbs one hit each level

* Rocket Boots

  * Grants one additional jump

* Fire Gloves

  * Start each level with a fire attack

* Lucky Charm

  * Slightly increases rare item drop rates

Equipment should encourage different playstyles rather than simply making players stronger.

## Progression

Players earn:

* Coins
* Experience
* Achievements
* Cosmetic unlocks
* New equipment
* Rare NFTs

Coins collected during gameplay can be used for upgrades, crafting, or other in-game mechanics.

## Rare Collectibles

Hidden throughout levels:

* Secret rooms
* Hidden exits
* Treasure chests
* Collectible artifacts
* Rare equipment
* Limited NFT rewards

Exploration should be rewarded.

## Multiplayer Features

Future updates could include:

* Speedrun leaderboards
* Weekly challenge levels
* Co-op levels
* Competitive races
* Community-created worlds
* Seasonal events

## Economy

The blockchain should support the game—not dominate it.

Players can:

* Own their characters permanently
* Trade NFTs
* Buy and sell equipment
* Collect rare items
* Upgrade equipment
* Craft new items
* Earn exclusive rewards through gameplay

Many of the best NFTs should be earned through achievements instead of purchased.

## Design Philosophy

This should not feel like a crypto game.

It should feel like an excellent platformer that happens to use blockchain technology for true ownership and long-term progression.

Players should enjoy it even if they know nothing about crypto.

The blockchain exists to provide persistent ownership, trading, and progression—not to replace fun gameplay.
